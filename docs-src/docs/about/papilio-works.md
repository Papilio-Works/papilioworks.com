# About Papilio Works

Papilio Works is the home of the Papilio Retrocade and the Papilio FPGA ecosystem — a revival of the original Gadget Factory brand from 2009–2019.

---

## The Story

Jack Gassett founded Gadget Factory in 2009 and spent ten years designing and selling FPGA hardware — Papilio boards, Wings, Arcade MegaWings, and the original Papilio Retrocade. Gadget Factory sold thousands of FPGA boards to makers, students, and hobbyists who wanted to learn FPGAs without the steep cost of Xilinx and Altera dev kits.

In 2019, Jack stepped back from Gadget Factory to work full-time at Kyndryl. But he never stopped tinkering. The Papilio Retrocade 20K is the result of that decade of experience combined with the new generation of affordable FPGA modules (Sipeed Tang series) and modern open-source retro cores.

**Papilio Works is Gadget Factory, rebuilt for 2026.**
