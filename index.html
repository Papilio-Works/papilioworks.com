Papilio Retrocade 20K

FPGA Retro Gaming,
Done Right

A modern FPGA development board built for retro computing and gaming. Run authentic hardware cores for the Atari 2600, Commodore 64, SNES and more — with WiFi OTA updates and wireless Bluetooth controllers, right out of the box.

Gowin GW2A-18 FPGA 128 MB DDR3 HDMI Output ESP32-S3 WiFi BLE Gamepad

Three Boards. One Platform.

The Retrocade system is built from three modular boards that work together.

01

Papilio Retrocade

The expansion daughterboard. Provides dedicated SDRAM, HDMI, audio, USB-C power, PMOD connector, and WS2812B RGB LED.

Designed by Papilio Works
02

Tang Primer 20K

The FPGA core module. Houses the Gowin GW2A-18 FPGA, 128 MB DDR3, and 27 MHz oscillator. Plugs into the Retrocade header.

Sipeed
03

ESP32-S3 SuperMini

The wireless brain. Handles WiFi OTA updates, Bluetooth gamepad pairing, OSD menu, SD card management, and USB HID.

Espressif

Classic Systems. Cycle-Accurate.

FPGA emulation runs authentic hardware logic — not software simulation. Every pixel, every clock cycle.

Built for the Modern Retro Gamer

📡

WiFi OTA Updates

Update firmware and bitfiles wirelessly. No programmer cables, no drivers — just your local network.

🎮

Wireless Gamepads

Pair Xbox Series, PS4, or PS5 controllers over Bluetooth. USB HID keyboards and gamepads also supported.

💾

SD Card Core Switching

Store multiple FPGA cores on a single SD card. Switch systems from the on-screen menu without reflashing.

📺

HDMI Video Output

Clean digital video to any HDMI display. No scan doublers, no converters needed.

🔊

3.5mm Audio

Analog stereo audio output. Plug into speakers or a headset for authentic retro sound.

🧩

PMOD Expansion

Standard PMOD connector for hardware expansion — add peripherals, debug with logic analyzers, or prototype new modules.

🔧

Onboard JTAG

No external programmer needed. Onboard JTAG gets you into the FPGA immediately.

🌈

RGB Status LED

WS2812B RGB LED for visual status feedback — see at a glance what core is loaded.
